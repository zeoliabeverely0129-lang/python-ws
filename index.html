The Challenges We Face

Climate Change

Rising greenhouse gas emissions are leading to global warming, melting glaciers, and frequent extreme weather events.

Plastic Crisis

Millions of tons of plastic enter our oceans every year, harming marine life and eventually entering the human food chain.

Deforestation

Forests are the "lungs of the Earth." Mass deforestation destroys biodiversity and accelerates global warming.
